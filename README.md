## 🚀 Project Summary

This project focuses on building a **dynamic HR Analytics Dashboard** using Power BI to uncover employee attrition patterns and workforce insights.

It simulates a real-world business scenario where HR teams need to **analyze employee data and make strategic decisions** to reduce attrition and improve organizational performance.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Employee attrition is a major challenge for organizations. High attrition leads to:

* Increased hiring costs
* Loss of experienced talent
* Reduced productivity

👉 This dashboard helps answer:

* Which departments have the highest attrition?
* Which age groups are most likely to leave?
* Which job roles are most affected?

---

## 📊 Key Insights (From Dashboard)

* 📌 Total Employees: **1,423**
* 📌 Total Attrition: **230 (~16% attrition rate)**
* 📌 Highest Attrition Age Group: **26–35 (111 employees)**
* 📌 Highest Attrition Department: **Research & Development (55.22%)**
* 📌 Top Job Role: **Sales Executive (318 employees)**
